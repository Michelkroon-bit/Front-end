Horizon Forbidden West

Horizon Forbidden West is an action role-playing game developed by Guerrilla Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It continues the story of Aloy, a hunter in a post-apocalyptic world dominated by machine creatures. The game features an expanded open world, including underwater exploration, and introduces new gameplay mechanics and enemies.

Above all, its moment-to-moment gameplay expertly combines the fun traversal, exciting combat, and creative problem-solving to awesome effect, meaning it always feels excellent to play.
