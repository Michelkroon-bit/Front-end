Marvel Spider-man 2

Marvel's Spider-Man 2 by Insomniac Games is an action-adventure game that continues the story of Peter Parker and Miles Morales as Spider-Men. Set in an expanded Marvel's New York, players can switch between the two heroes to experience unique abilities and narratives. The game introduces iconic villains like Venom, Kraven the Hunter, and the Lizard, while offering enhanced traversal and combat mechanics, including new web-based gadgets and suits2. It’s a thrilling sequel that builds on the foundation of its predecessors.
